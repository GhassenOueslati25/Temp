About Al-Gannas Qatari Society

Al-Gannas Qatari Society is a cultural association specialized in falconry. It aims to encourage the Arabian traditional hunting by supporting falconers and managing their affairs. Al-Gannas hasorganized numerous events and activities, and represented Arab falconers in regional and global forums.
Al-Gannas various interests include hunting with falcons, saluqi (hunting dogs) and rifles todevelop traditional hunting on scientific basis and to introduce falconry to the new generation.In 2010, UNESCO declared falconry as an intangible heritage of humanity, several countries wereassociated with the inscription of this element, including:
Qatar, Saudi Arabia, United Arab Emirates, Belgium, The Czech Replublic, France, Republic of Korea,Mongollia, Morocco, Spain and The Syrian Arab Republic.

Our Mission

The mission of Al-Gannas Society is to organize traditional falconry by increasing membership base locally and regionally. It also aims to develop methods and regulations to govern falconry by providing services and organizing events and competitons and encouraging participation, research and studies within this field.

In order to enable us to carry out this role with success and distinction Al-Gannas has excellent facilities and equipment .

Al-Gannas Building

Al-Gannas building is located in the Katara cultural village, Building No.33, West Bay area of Doha, Qatar. the building consists of a falconry equipment exhibition, lecture theater for seminars, a veterinary clinic, library related to falconry books and archives, and the administrative offices.

Our Goals

  • 1.  Achieve Qatar national Vision for the year 2030 .
  • 2.  Raising the awareness and improving the quality of falconry , saluqi and rifle hunting .
  • 3.  Hosting seminars ans producing publications clarifying falconry regulations both inside and outside the state .
  • 4.  Communicating with International organizations and associations specialized in relevant activities .
  • 5.  Establishment of a specialized research center .
  • 6.  Providing the necessary information to facilitate participation in Al-Gannas Society ,Increasing its membership base and providing the logistical services the members demand .
  • 7.  Providing veterinary care and Establishment of veterinary hospital to address the health needs of falcon and saluqi .
  • 8.  Coordinating relashionship with other institutions both locally and globally in order to prevent over hunting and to preserve
    nature reserves inside and outside Qatar with the support of the state .
  • 9.  Organizing internal festivals within the state and participating in international events and festivals to represent Arab Falconry .
  • 10.  Publicizing the knowledge of falcons, their habits, life cycles, species,place of origin and migration routes .
  • 11.  Organizing multiple courses for beginners to learn the basic principals of the world of falconry, hunting,
    nutrition, disease prevention, habits and behaviour of falcons .
  • 12.  Organizing trips for young falconers to familarize them with the concept of hunting as a sport and to cultivate and transfer ancestral heritage to future generations .
