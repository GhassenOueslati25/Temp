Qatar's International Veterinary
Falcon Conference

Qatar's Interational Veterinary Falcon Conference (FalCon) is a pioneer global forum to review the latest advances in the field of falcon veterinary, and a unique opportunity for veteranaries, scientists, researchers, falconers and relevant governement and non-governement organizations to address providing falcons with scientifically-based care and discuss the cutting-edge research and literature in this field.
